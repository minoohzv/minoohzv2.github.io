About Me

I am Minoo Hezaveh, and this is my 1st semester at Algonquin College. I arrived in Canada two years ago and over this period, I studied English at uOttawa as an ESL student. I love all different types of art, including photography, handicrafts, and painting. I also really enjoy web development and programming, and I wish to improve my skills through this course. I believe that web development allows me to express myself creatively while also utilizing my technical proficiency. I have an undergraduate degree in biotechnology; however, it is not a field I enjoy. For that reason, I decided to follow my interest and passion, which has led me here.

Life is to be enjoyed, not endured. So follow your dreams, embrace change and live what you love. - Aysel Gunar

In my free time I like to learn and practice coding. In fact, I started to learn coding through self-study. My favorite languages are HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Thus far, I have followed YouTube tutorials and programming websites to learn more about programming languages.

I love both digital and traditional painting. My favorite digital art application is Adobe Illustrator because it allows me to express my creativity. Moreover, I have been an oil painter since I was 9 years old. I am familiar with choosing colours, and I know where and how to use each colour.
